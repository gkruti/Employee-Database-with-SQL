1. List the following details of each employee: employee number, last name, first name, sex, and salary.

SELECT emp.emp_num, emp.last_name, emp.first_name, emp.sex, salaries.salary
FROM employees
INNER JOIN salaries ON
emp.emp_num = salaries.emp_num;



2. List first name, last name, and hire date for employees who were hired in 1986.

SELECT emp.first_name, emp.last_name, emp.hire_date
FROM employees
WHERE hire_date('year', hire_date) = 1986

8. In descending order, list the frequency count of employee last names,
 i.e., how many employees share each last name.

SELECT last_name, COUNT(freq_last_name)
FROM employees
GROUP BY last_name
ORDER BY COUNT(freq_last_name) DESC;
